Ecology

  • Definition: Study of interactions between living organisms and their environment.
  • Subdivisions:
    • Autecology ‚Üí study of one species.
    • Synecology ‚Üí study of communities of organisms.
  • Ecosystem: Interactions between organisms and physical environment.
  • Symbiosis:
    • Mutualism ‚Üí both benefit.
    • Commensalism ‚Üí one benefits, other unaffected.
    • Parasitism ‚Üí one benefits, one harmed.
    • Predation ‚Üí predator vs prey.
  • Factors:
    • Abiotic ‚Üí light, temperature, water, soil.
    • Biotic ‚Üí plants, animals, microbes.
    • Limiting factors control population size.
  • Levels of Organization: Individual ‚Üí Population ‚Üí Community ‚Üí Ecosystem ‚Üí Biosphere.

Environmental Engineering

  • Applies science & math to solve sanitation and environmental problems.
  • Sanitary Works:
    1. Safe and ample water supply.
    2. Wastewater and solid waste disposal/recycling.
    3. Drainage of rural & urban areas.
    4. Control of pollution (water, soil, air).
    5. Social & environmental impact assessment.
