Ecology

  • Definition: Study of interactions between living organisms and their environment.
  • Subdivisions: Autecology (one species), Synecology (communities).
  • Ecosystem: Organisms + physical environment.
  • Symbiosis: Mutualism, Commensalism, Parasitism, Predation.
  • Factors: Abiotic (light, temp, soil) & Biotic (plants, animals, microbes).
  • Levels of Organization: Individual ‚Üí Population ‚Üí Community ‚Üí Ecosystem ‚Üí Biosphere.
